Vinyl Fence Care

Installation Guidelines

Be sure to follow correct installation guidelines. Avoid exposing your vinyl fence to unusual or high heat sources like fire pits, BBQ grills, or reflective surfaces such as windows and glass doors. Also avoid placing vinyl fencing next to heat-retaining materials like asphalt, concrete, or dark-colored gravel.

Heat Management

Darker finishes—like Golden Oak or Red Chestnut—can become warm to the touch under long-term sunlight exposure. To help reduce heat buildup, consider using awnings, umbrellas, or similar shading solutions.

Cleaning

To clean off dust or dirt, simply use mild soap and water. Always test any cleaner in a small, hidden section first to ensure it doesn't affect the vinyl fence's appearance.

Important Warnings

Do NOT paint your vinyl fencing. Products like spray-on sunscreen or insect repellent may stain the surface if sprayed directly on or near the vinyl. Apply these products indoors, away from your fence and railing, or take care to avoid overspray. Avoid placing rubber mats directly on vinyl surfaces, as they can lead to discoloration over time.

PrivacyGuard Vinyl Fence

Unlike standard tongue-and-groove styles, our exclusive PrivacyGuard system secures each vinyl panel with a locking connection. This design prevents wind from passing through and significantly lowers the risk of fence damage compared to traditional vinyl fence styles. With PrivacyGuard, your fence provides reliable security.

WeatherSeal UV Protection

The sun's harsh rays can fade and discolor ordinary vinyl fences over time. That's why every Dollar Fence vinyl fence includes WeatherSeal — a proprietary acrylic blend designed to block UV exposure. WeatherSeal acts as a durable outer layer, reducing oxidation and maintaining your fence's original finish even in tough climates. With WeatherSeal, your fence gets advanced sun protection.

Our StrongMan rail system includes grooves that are 32% deeper than standard rails, allowing pickets to embed more securely. This structural advantage improves wind resistance and overall durability. With StrongMan, your vinyl fence delivers unmatched strength in all conditions.
