Wrought Iron vs. Steel Fence

At Dollar Fence, we often hear customers use "wrought iron" and "steel" fences interchangeably, but they are not the same. Wrought iron fences are hand-forged from raw iron and crafted one piece at a time, which makes them costly and labour-intensive. Steel fences, by contrast, are made from iron alloyed with carbon and manufactured for a consistent finish. With steel fencing, you get the elegant appearance of wrought iron—without the premium cost.
