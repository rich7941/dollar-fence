Chain Link Fence

Chain link fence provides strength and long-lasting durability at an affordable cost. Dollar Fence is your go-to chain link fence company. This fence style is crafted from woven steel wire formed into a diamond pattern, then finished with either a zinc galvanised coating or a protective vinyl layer. Its cost-effective design makes it ideal for both residential and commercial use, while its strong security features make it a preferred option for state and local government properties.

Dollar Fence is one of the largest nationwide suppliers of chain link fence. We maintain a comprehensive inventory of chain link fences ideal for residential homes, outdoor tennis or basketball courts, school playgrounds, farms, and various industrial properties.

Chain link fence is also a popular solution for temporary needs such as construction sites, and we offer chain link fences for rental use as well. Additionally, we serve pool installers, commercial contractors, and general builders with wholesale and bulk chain link fence supply.
