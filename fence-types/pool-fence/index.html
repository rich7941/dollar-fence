Dollar Fence delivers the safest pool fencing available

By law, pool fences must be at least 48 inches high. However, the code refers to the installed height — not just the panel height. Dollar Fence ensures your finished pool fence installation meets or exceeds this requirement. Our vinyl and aluminum pool fence panels are built taller than 48 inches so that, once installed, they fully comply with code. Dollar Fence pool fences are also designed without footholds or handholds. Vertical pickets are spaced no more than 4 inches apart, and horizontal rails are positioned at least 45 inches apart to prevent climbing. For wood pool fences, if the "good side" faces outward, we still position supports to meet the same spacing rule. The bottom of the fence remains within 4 inches of solid ground or 2 inches for soft surfaces like grass or gravel.
