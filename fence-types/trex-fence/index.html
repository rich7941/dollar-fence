Trex Fence Systems

Trex Fence Care

Installation Guidelines

Follow proper installation guidelines. Avoid placing your Trex fence near high heat sources such as fire pits, grills, or glass reflections.

Heat Management

Darker Trex colors like Golden Oak or Red Chestnut may warm up in direct sun—use awnings or shade structures to mitigate heat retention.

Cleaning

Clean dirt with mild soap and water. Always test cleaning agents on a hidden section before full application.

Important Warnings

Do not paint Trex fencing. Products like bug spray or sunscreen can cause discoloration if sprayed directly. Apply these away from the fence. Avoid rubber mats near the base, as they may cause surface discoloration.
