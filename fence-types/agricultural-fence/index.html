This lumber is protected by warranties against rot and termite damage, giving you peace of mind for years to come.

3 & 4 Board Ranch Rail Fence

Our 3-rail and 4-rail wood fences are a classic choice among farmers and ranchers. These fences are widely seen across rural properties and remain the standard for the iconic "Kentucky-style" farm fence look. Strong, reliable, and timeless.

Wood Horizontal Slat Agricultural Fence

For a sleeker profile, our horizontal slat fence offers a modern take on the classic ranch style. With more boards and smaller gaps than traditional 3- or 4-board fences, it creates a tighter, streamlined appearance. This option is popular among livestock owners and equestrian centres looking for a blend of safety and aesthetics.

Iron Agricultural Fencing

For maximum longevity, our iron post-and-rail fencing is made from 100% powder-coated steel. It's the most durable low-maintenance agricultural fence available today. Rails are crafted from 18-gauge steel, posts from 16-gauge, offering 1,200 lbs. of vertical strength and 400 lbs. of horizontal force resistance—with 4 inches of flexibility for animal safety.

Low-Maintenance Vinyl Agricultural Fencing

In addition to traditional wood options, Dollar Fence also installs vinyl ranch rail fencing for a clean, low-maintenance solution. Vinyl fencing maintains clear visibility across your property line and is ideal for farms, stables, and livestock enclosures. Our standard vinyl ranch rail comes in a 3-rail setup but can be adjusted to a 2-rail configuration if needed.
